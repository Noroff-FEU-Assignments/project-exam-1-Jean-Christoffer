Who are we?

Wave is a well-established electronic music blog that has been around for a decade. It has been providing music lovers with the latest and greatest in electronic music, covering a broad spectrum of genres, from techno and house to experimental and ambient. The blog has gained a following of both seasoned veterans of the electronic music scene and newcomers alike, who are looking to explore the genre.

Want to write for us? We are always looking for new talent, Contact us!

Contact us

Over the past 10 years, Wave has showcased emerging artists and underground sounds that might not have received as much attention otherwise. This dedication to promoting new talent and unique sounds has helped to establish the blog as a go-to destination for electronic music fans.

With a team of dedicated writers, Wave covers a diverse range of content, including interviews with artists, album reviews, event coverage, and playlists. The blog also provides its readers with a space to discover new music, offering curated playlists that highlight the latest and most innovative tracks.
